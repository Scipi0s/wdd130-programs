Fire Safety Job Descriptions

Fire Safety: Student Lead

Fire Safety Student Lead Description: The Fire Safety Student Lead position is a role in which you act as a supervisor under the direction of the Fire Safety Coordinator. In addition to the routine inspections of fire suppression systems in your route, you also will be tasked with maintaining communication with the other students on your team and passing along any concerns or updates that they may have to your superior for review. The Fire Safety Student Lead also assists in coordinating and setting up Fire Watches, assisting the Fire Safety Coordinator in hiring new employees to the team, coordinating and reviewing team schedules as well as making sure Hot Work Permits and inspections are completed in a timely manner.

Fire Safety: Student

Fire Safety Student Description: In the Fire Safety Student position your primary roles are to perform weekly and monthly inspections of the school's fire extinguishers and fire sprinkler valves. You also will assist in performing patrols during event Fire Watches as well as assist the Student Lead and Fire Safety Coordinator in any projects that they need assistance in.

Fire Safety: Hazmat

Fire Safety Hazmat Description: In the Fire Safety Hazmat position, your primary role is to perform maintenance on fire extinguishers that require any 6- or 12-year inspections or are damaged in some way. Once maintenance is performed on the affected extinguishers you then replace these extinguishers in their proper locations. Like the Fire Safety Student position, you will also assist the Student Lead and Fire Safety Coordinator in associated projects as well as assisting in Fire Watches and inspection routes as necessary.
